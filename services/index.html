    E-COMMERCE SOLUTIONS

    Myfreight seamlessly integrates with a number of popular ecommerce platforms, including Shopify, Magento and WooCommerce. Built in house, our integrations can be configured to suit your businesses individual ecommerce needs. Our operations and software development teams support our customers changing and configuring their ecommerce setup to find the best solution for their business. Myfreight offers a suite of supplementary products to create an B2C experience that keeps the consumer informed on all stages of delivery. This includes online tracking pages and shipping notices via email and SMS which can be customised and branded for your business.

    SUPPLIER AND RETURN FREIGHT SOLUTIONS

    For Myfreight customers with a large quantity of suppliers and cost responsibility for inbound freight, Myfreight has developed a specific portal for suppliers to securely generate consignments, print labels and automatically book collections online. The portal provides cost and service control for inbound shipments as well as mechanisms to secure access to your transport accounts and optional authentication of purchase order numbers. Suppliers must be approved before accessing the portal and can be given individual permissions for shipping. All elements of the Supplier Freight portal including the URL, website images, text, colour and logos are customised to promote client brand recognition.
